About Me

Hi, I'm Blake Watton-Dirkse from South Africa. My journey into coding started during high school when I began experimenting with code to unlock in-game items for free. Inspired by seeing others in the field and the rapid growth of the tech industry, especially with the shift to online business post-COVID, I dove deeper into coding.
Currently, I’m working towards becoming a full-stack engineer. Though I’m still gaining experience, I’m passionate about tech and excited about the future. Outside of coding, I enjoy working out, spending time with loved ones, playing pool, gaming, drawing, and reading. I’m also a car enthusiast with dreams of traveling the world, owning the cars I admire, and living a fulfilling life.
